This setup provides a mental math challenge using 8 random two-digit numbers arranged clockwise.

## Game Mechanics

- The Goal: Work your way around the circle by summing consecutive pairs.
- The Current Task: The red arrow highlights the first addition: $80 + 13$.
- Interaction: Enter the correct sum ($93$) into the center box to advance the arrow to the next pair ($13 + 34$).
